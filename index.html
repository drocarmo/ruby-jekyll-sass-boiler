Wheat, a Jekyll starter-template for designers.

Wheat is a great starting point to help any designer to quickly wip out a simple content-managed site, a product marketing page, prototype an idea or even learn more about Jekyll. The hope is that you may find it easy to quickly iterate whilst designing in the browser.

Here are a few examples of sites built with Wheat:

ChicagoWebConf — Product Design Resources — Elenco App

git clone https://github.com/drocarmo/wheat.git
